1. Executive Intelligence Brief

The Post-GenAI Transformation

The AI/ML tooling landscape has undergone the most radical transformation in technology history between 2022 and 2025. What began as a stable stack—PyTorch + cloud platform + MLflow—has exploded into fourteen distinct layers encompassing entirely new categories: vector databases, LLM gateways, AI agent frameworks, and AI safety tooling[1].

The #1 Finding

The modern AI stack has bifurcated into two incompatible ecosystems—English-language/Western and Chinese—with minimal cross-pollution, creating the largest information asymmetry and competitive blindspot in global technology markets.

Velocity of Change

New tools launch weekly; category leaders from 2023 face displacement by 2025. The research methodology explicitly recognizes this as "the MOST RAPIDLY EVOLVING domain in all of technology."

Open Source Dominance

80%+ of the stack is open source (PyTorch, Hugging Face, LangChain), creating extraordinary competitive pressure where commercial tools must demonstrate clear superiority over free alternatives.

GenAI Category Creation

Entirely new categories emerged 2022-2025: vector databases ($2B+ funding), AI agent frameworks, RAG tools, prompt management systems—all now essential infrastructure.

  • Risk mitigation from provider outages
  • Open-source for cost-sensitive, high-volume applications

10. Special Focus Areas

The "LLM Wrapper" Problem

Critical Risk Assessment

"Hundreds of tools are thin wrappers around OpenAI/Anthropic APIs" creating existential commoditization risk as underlying APIs evolve and improve[1].

Wrapper Identification Framework

High Risk Indicators
  • • Single API provider dependency
  • • UI-only value addition
  • • No proprietary data or model
  • • No network effects or switching costs
  • • Rapidly replicable by API evolution
Genuine Differentiation
  • • Multi-provider optimization
  • • Proprietary data flywheel
  • • Deep workflow integration
  • • Community/content ecosystem
  • • Technical moat and expertise

Survival Strategies
